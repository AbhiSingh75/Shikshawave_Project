================================================================================
STATISTICS BREAKDOWN
================================================================================

1. TOTAL ACTIVE STUDENTS
   - Shows count of students with IsDeleted = 0
   - Primary metric
   - Used for percentage calculations

2. MALE STUDENTS
   - Count of male students
   - Shows percentage of total
   - Example: "45 (52.3%)"

3. FEMALE STUDENTS
   - Count of female students
   - Shows percentage of total
   - Example: "41 (47.7%)"

4. GENERAL CATEGORY
   - Count of General category students
   - India's general category

5. OBC CATEGORY
   - Count of OBC (Other Backward Classes)
   - India's reservation category

6. SC CATEGORY
   - Count of SC (Scheduled Castes)
   - India's reservation category

7. ST CATEGORY
   - Count of ST (Scheduled Tribes)
   - India's reservation category

8. INACTIVE STUDENTS
   - Count of deleted/inactive students
   - Shows IsDeleted = 1

================================================================================
VISUAL DESIGN
================================================================================

COLOR SCHEME:
- Background: Purple gradient (#667eea to #764ba2)
- Cards: White semi-transparent (15% opacity)
- Text: White with varying opacity
- Icons: White with 70% opacity

LAYOUT:
- Responsive grid (auto-fit, min 150px)
- 15px gap between cards
- Rounded corners (10px for cards, 15px for main)
- Box shadow for depth

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Title: 24px, bold, white
- Subtitle: 14px, 80% opacity
- Count: 28px, bold, white
- Label: 13px, 90% opacity
- Percentage: 11px, 70% opacity
